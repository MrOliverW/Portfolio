About Me

Hello and welcome, thank you for being here today! My name is Oliver Warlick.

I love software engineering and business development. Currenly I am an intern at Bezos Academy on the engineering team. I am also finishing up a yearlong application development program with the YearUp non-profit organization.

My journey as a webdveloper began when I built my first website using GoDaddy.com about 6 years ago; I decided to add some HTML to my website and learned that GoDaddy only allows limited site customization.

I wanted more room for creativity with my web design so I switched to WordPress; an open source platform and from there quickly learned just how much there is to learn about web development. YearUp offered the opportunity to learn and earn and has been a great supporter in my career path.

I am very grateful to all my mentors, techers, coaches, workplace professionals, fellow classmates, friends and family, who have supported me on my path. I feel very fortunate to have the opportunity to advance my career in the tech industry.
